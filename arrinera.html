What’s New for the 2025 Arrinera Hussarya

The Arrinera Hussarya combines raw power with precision engineering, delivering a truly track-focused driving experience. With its naturally aspirated V8 engine, the Hussarya offers exhilarating acceleration and a top-tier performance setup designed for the most demanding drivers.

The cockpit features a driver-centered design, advanced telemetry, and lightweight materials that enhance control and responsiveness. Its aerodynamic body, active rear wing, and racing-grade suspension ensure every drive is both thrilling and precise, making the Hussarya a true hypercar built for performance enthusiasts.
